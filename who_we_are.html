About Us

Easysauces Nigeria Limited (ESNL)

Easysauces Nigeria Limited (ESNL) is a wholly owned indigenous company incorporated in Nigeria under the Corporate Affairs Commission (C.A.C) with RC1134678. Its objective is to manufacture, market, distribute and sell African sauces and other tomato based sauces to local and international markets.

The business will develop an organic blend of simple raw materials of the highest standard quality-wise, that will appeal to indigenous and global markets. This will create jobs for Nigerians, encourage domestic and foreign investment and increase earnings for local farmers holistically.

Mission

To be the foremost developer of value added African foods, to create healthy and good quality processed foods and market same globally.
